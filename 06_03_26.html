JUN 03 2026 · WED

Iran · Day 95. Kuwait airport struck. Mutual attacks escalating. Iranian drones struck Kuwait's main airport Wednesday, killing one Indian national and injuring more than 60 others — Kuwait briefly shut the airport. The attack came after the US military used a Hellfire missile to hit a Botswana-flagged oil tanker heading toward Iran's Kharg Island port. Iran said it also targeted US bases in Kuwait and Bahrain and a vessel near the Strait of Hormuz. Iran stopped communicating with mediators about extending the ceasefire. A regional official: Tehran wants Lebanon enforced before returning to talks. Trump said negotiations continue and declared it is "time, one way or another" for Iran to make a deal. Sources: CNN, NPR, Time.

Lebanon. Israeli forces have moved deeper into Lebanon than at any time in over 25 years — despite the nominal ceasefire. Israeli drones killed at least 8 people Wednesday. Trump described as "perturbed" with Netanyahu. Netanyahu wants to keep Iran and Lebanon issues separate, under heavy domestic pressure ahead of fall elections. Iran's position: "The ceasefire on all fronts is a ceasefire. Its violation on one front is a violation on all fronts." Sources: NPR, Democracy Now, CNN.

Ukraine · Day 1560. Russia bombarded Ukraine overnight, killing at least 18. Drone and missile attacks on Kyiv on June 2. Russia total losses ~1,366,000+. Source: NBC News.

Ebola · Kenya backlash. Violent protests erupted in Kenya over the US plan to quarantine Ebola-exposed Americans there. Kenya's President Ruto defended the proposed 50-bed facility. US health experts warned of "profound clinical, ethical, operational, and legal concerns." Kenyan court suspension of the plan remains active. Source: NBC News.

US domestic. Primary elections in six states today: California (governor race attracting national attention), Iowa, Montana, New Jersey, New Mexico, South Dakota. The fight to shape the future of the Democratic Party playing out. Source: NBC News.
