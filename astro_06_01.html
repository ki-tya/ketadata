GEMINI SUN · URANUS CONJUNCT SEASON
You are operating inside a year when information infrastructure is actively breaking down and being rebuilt. Uranus in Gemini — which happens roughly every 84 years — disrupts the systems through which knowledge moves: media, language, communication networks, the categories you use to organise what you know. Gemini collects information; Uranus breaks the containers it's stored in. The practical implication: what you think you know about the world should be held lightly right now. Not with paralysis, but with flexibility. The signal is real. The frame is temporary.
SATURN IN ARIES · THE STRUCTURE OF ACTION
Saturn entered Aries in mid-2025 and will be here until late 2027. This is a period when the cost of acting without thinking becomes visible very fast. Aries wants to move. Saturn wants proof that the structure can hold. The tension between urgency and discipline is the defining pressure of the next two years. For anyone building something — any kind of real infrastructure, physical or conceptual — Saturn in Aries is demanding: does this hold under pressure? Build accordingly.
PLUTO IN AQUARIUS · COLLECTIVE TRANSFORMATION
Pluto will stay in Aquarius until 2043. This is a 20-year arc. The last time Pluto was in Aquarius was 1778–1798 — the period of the American and French Revolutions, the Industrial Revolution beginning, the reorganisation of collective power structures. The current period carries similar energy: the transformation of who holds power within collective systems — networks, platforms, institutions, states. AI, surveillance capitalism, the breakdown of legacy media, sovereign data environments — these are all Pluto-in-Aquarius phenomena. The question this placement asks: what does collective power look like when the architecture changes?
JUPITER IN LEO · EXPANSION THROUGH VISIBILITY
Jupiter in Leo, from mid-2025 through mid-2026, expands the territory of creative identity, personal authority, and visibility. This is a year when what you make and who you are as a maker are in alignment. Leo's shadow is performance — doing it for the room rather than from the work. Jupiter's shadow is overextension. The right use of this energy: build with genuine creative confidence, not audience capture. The work knows the difference.
NORTH NODE IN PISCES · THE COLLECTIVE DIRECTION
The North Node in Pisces (with South Node in Virgo) from 2025 through mid-2026 is asking collectively: can you release the need to analyse, perfect, and classify everything in order to simply be present? Virgo is the intelligence that breaks reality into manageable systems. Pisces is the intelligence that accepts reality as it is, without forcing it into categories. The tension between these two — actuality vs. framework, presence vs. system — is the edge the current moment is sitting on. Both are real. The node says the growth is toward the Pisces side right now.
