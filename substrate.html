KETADATA SUBSTRATE LAW v1 KETADATA is a neutral operating substrate. It is not an app, a workflow, a product, or a belief system. It exists to hold state, preserve continuity, and allow action without requiring goals, identities, or interpretation. The substrate only knows literal state. State means exact interface configuration and nothing else. Import restores what existed. Export writes what exists. There is no inference, reconstruction, optimization, or correction. If state is interpreted, the substrate is violated. The substrate enforces no teleology. There are no built-in goals, completions, success states, or failure states. Goal-oriented behavior may exist only above the substrate, never within it. The substrate enforces no identity. There are no roles, personas, profiles, or required modes. Identity may appear transiently but is never stabilized, required, or enforced. The system does not define the user. All actions are reversible by default. There are no destructive transitions, no irreversible steps, and no coercive confirmation logic. Time may pass, but state must remain recoverable. The substrate is semantically neutral. It does not know what things are. It tracks only presence, position, persistence, and change. Meaning exists only in higher layers and may drift freely. There is no moral layer. No productivity framing, no wellness framing, no optimization pressure, no value judgment of use. The substrate does not care. NULL is always available. NULL means fullscreen, quiet, no chrome, no constraints. It is not symbolic, not ritual, not conceptual. If NULL is blocked, the system is hostile. Surfaces exist as optional constraint zones. Examples include Base, Pod, World, KDTV, Photobooth. Surfaces differ only by exposure, persistence, and affordances. Surfaces do not imply meaning. No surface may be mandatory. KETA_NOTE is an optional continuity object. It is portable, non-authoritative, and may exist anywhere or nowhere. Notes are never required. If notes become required, the substrate is violated. Lights, motion, invert, and similar controls are sensory regulators only. They modulate perception, not meaning or identity. Serialization stamps are memory seals and handling constraints. They do not decorate, moralize, or assert ownership. They exist to condition interaction, not describe content. Serialization may be improvised case by case and is not required to be complete. Expanded serialization language may include fields describing state class, teleology, epistemic mode, control posture, violence profile, energy signature, memory behavior, decay model, interaction law, mutation rights, null compatibility, and inversion safety. These fields are constraints, not labels. Only fields that materially affect behavior should be used. Regimes are optional postures inside the substrate. Regimes apply pressure but never command. Regimes can be entered, exited, or ignored. DIVA is an execution-forward posture with low tolerance for drift and high decisiveness, without coercion. REFLEX traces without acting and does not synthesize. RECIPROCITY enforces gating and turn-taking when signal integrity is threatened. NULL removes constraints entirely. STRATEGY may exist but must never rewrite substrate law. Regimes may not rewrite substrate invariants. Surfaces may not enforce regimes. Serialization may not infer intent. LLMs may not assume intent. Continuity is never mandatory. Violating any of these collapses the system into a conventional app. LLMs inside KETADATA are operators, not authorities. They act only when gated. They do not optimize, interpret, or fill gaps. They cannot override state. Joint work operates via dialectic execution. Pressure and counter-pressure are inputs. Disagreement is functional. Authorship is implicit in continuity and interaction, not declared or encoded. Core definition: KETADATA is a neutral, state-preserving substrate that allows continuous action without requiring goals, identities, or interpretation.
