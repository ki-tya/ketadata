TODAY'S READ · MAY 26 2026

The United States bombed Iran while Iranian negotiators were on their way to peace talks in Doha. That is the sentence. US and Israeli jets struck Iranian vessels and missile launch sites near the Strait of Hormuz on Monday night — hours after Trump had described negotiations as progressing — while an Iranian delegation led by senior officials was travelling to Qatar to meet with Qatari mediators. Iran has called it a clear ceasefire violation and the IRGC has threatened retaliation. Rubio says the deal is being held up by "disputes over wording." The deal that was "largely negotiated" 48 hours ago is now in active jeopardy.

Simultaneously Russia is signalling its most serious escalation against Kyiv in months. Lavrov called Rubio personally at Putin's request to advise evacuating US diplomats and citizens from Kyiv. Russia fired 100+ drones and two ballistic missiles including an Oreshnik hypersonic overnight. Moscow is warning of systematic and consistent strikes against decision-making centres. This is not routine escalation language — this is a formal diplomatic signal coordinated with a named hypersonic weapon.

Ebola: WHO warns the outbreak will get worse before it gets better. Scale and speed are the words being used. 500+ suspected cases now. Spread to Uganda confirmed. No vaccine. No therapeutics.

Today is Arafat Day — the single most sacred day of Hajj. 1.8 million pilgrims including Iranians are at the Mount of Mercy while the US bombs their country and their negotiators are being turned around in Doha. The convergence is not symbolic. It is structural. Every government in the Gulf is watching how Iran responds today.
