Iran · Day 91. US and Iranian negotiators have reached a tentative 60-day ceasefire extension MOU. Trump has not yet approved. Iran's Tasnim News Agency: text not finalized or confirmed by Iran. Deal terms per Axios (confirmed Bloomberg, MSNBC, Democracy Now): 60-day extension, nuclear negotiations begin during period, Hormuz reopens, Iran has 30 days to remove mines, no tolls on passing ships. No upfront nuclear concessions from Iran — HEU stockpile and enrichment deferred. Vance: "very close." Eurasia Group analyst Gregory Brew: Iran wins four ways, US wins one.

Ukraine · Day 1556. Russian drone crashed into an apartment building in eastern Romania on Friday, injuring two people. Romania is a NATO member. CNN (published today, May 30): Ukraine has conducted 22,000 unmanned robot operations since January alone — veterans directing from gamer chairs, robots called "silent death" by Russian prisoners of war. Saab considering Canadian plant for Gripen production for Ukraine. Ukraine blocking Crimea-Donetsk land corridor.

Ebola. Wikipedia as of May 29: 1,262 suspected and confirmed cases, at least 241 deaths. New detection finding (Red Cross, May 23): three workers who died May 5–16 are believed to have contracted Ebola on March 27 during dead body management in Mongwalu — 40 days before the first known death was identified. WHO Director General arrived in Kinshasa on May 29 to witness response. US State Dept total commitment: $162M+ direct plus $350M through OCHA. $13.5M for Kenya preparedness. No US cases.

Climate. UN/Met Office projection (May 29): global surface temperatures will exceed 1.5°C above pre-industrial levels — passing the Paris Agreement limit. Portugal recorded its hottest-ever May day. London: 95°F for two consecutive days.
