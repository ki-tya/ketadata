MAY 29 2026 · FRI

Iran · Day 90. US and Iranian negotiators have reached a preliminary 60-day memorandum of understanding to extend the ceasefire and open negotiations on Iran's nuclear program. Trump has not yet approved. Sources: Bloomberg (confirmed by person with knowledge), Axios (first reported), MSNBC (US sources). The deal does not resolve the central issues — Iran's highly enriched uranium stockpile and enrichment rights — but defers them to the negotiation period. Vance (CNN, May 28): "There are a couple of issues on the nuclear stuff, the highly enriched stockpile, and also the question of enrichment... I feel pretty good about it" but "can't guarantee." Eurasia Group analyst Gregory Brew: "Overall this is a victory for Iran, which endured over a month of bombing, closed the strait, kept it closed and only agreed to reopen it via negotiated settlement."

Ebola — escalating. Updated figures as of May 28 (ECDC, May 29): DRC 121 confirmed cases (17 deaths), 1,077 suspected cases (246 deaths). Uganda: 7 confirmed, 1 dead. Uganda has closed its border with DRC — only essential traffic permitted. US Embassy Kampala issued a Level 4 ("Do Not Travel") advisory for Uganda on May 28. CDC raised Uganda Travel Health Notice from Level 1 to Level 2 on May 27. State Department allocated $300 million through OCHA and an additional $80 million in bilateral assistance (finalized May 27). One American tested positive for Ebola on May 17 — CDC working to evacuate from DRC.

Ukraine · Day 1555. Russia total losses as of May 28: ~1,360,110 (+1,160 in past day). 256 combat engagements recorded May 27 — nearly 50 attacks concentrated in the Pokrovsk sector. GCHQ assessment: "nearly half a million" Russian soldiers killed since February 2022. ISW April 21–May 19: Russia net loss of 69 square miles of Ukrainian territory.

OPEN QUESTIONS
TRUMP APPROVAL
The 60-day MOU was reached by negotiators. Trump has not signed off. He has previously indicated being close to a deal multiple times without approving. Vance: "the president will be in a position where he can endorse the agreement, but obviously that's still TBD." The gap between negotiator agreement and presidential approval is where the previous deal frameworks have collapsed.
DEFERRED ISSUES
The MOU defers Iran's HEU stockpile and enrichment rights to the negotiation period. These are the same issues that have blocked a deal for 90 days. Deferral extends the ceasefire — it does not resolve the conflict. Eurasia Group: this is a victory for Iran on terms because the strait was kept closed until negotiated settlement was agreed.
EBOLA AMERICAN EVACUATION
CDC is working to move an American who tested positive for Ebola on May 17 and other Americans at high risk out of DRC. Confirmed by CDC situation summary (May 27). No confirmed evacuation timeline in available sources.
UGANDA BORDER CLOSURE
Uganda closed its border with DRC as of ~May 28. Only essential traffic permitted. This is a significant containment action. It also affects trade and movement in a region where population and trade flows are described by WHO as a primary transmission risk factor.
