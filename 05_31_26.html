MAY 31 2026 · SUN

Iran · Day 92. Trump's Situation Room meeting on Friday to make a "final determination" on the Iran MOU ended after two hours with no decision (NYT, Times of Israel). White House official to AFP: "President Trump will only make a deal that is good for America and satisfies his red lines." Trump subsequently toughened terms and sent a new framework back to Iran — NYT reports the changes, though specific new terms not confirmed. Tehran "may take days to respond" per US officials. Tehran denies the agreement includes nuclear concessions. Hegseth: "Iran doesn't want to give up nuclear ambitions, but we think it will, over time, with right incentives." In a separate social media post, Trump told ships in the Strait they could "start the process of heading home" — suggesting he has already decided to lift the naval blockade, which per leaked MOU terms was supposed to happen only after signing.

Ukraine · Day 1557. Russia total losses: ~1,364,060, up by 1,560 in past 24 hours (Ukrinform). Russia carried out 976 attacks on 55 settlements in Zaporizhzhia region — 2 killed, 7 injured. Russia Matters ISW data (April 28–May 26): Russia lost a net 100 square miles of Ukrainian territory in four weeks — its largest monthly loss this year. Zelenskyy ordered preparations for two to three more years of war (The Economist). Separately: US reportedly planning to accelerate withdrawal of troops from European bases, to present proposals to NATO allies next month (Germany's Welt am Sonntag).

Ebola. WHO Director General Tedros travelled to Ituri province — the outbreak's epicentre — on Saturday, urging early treatment-seeking and safe burial practices. The most recent confirmed case figures remain May 29 (Wikipedia: 1,262 suspected/confirmed, 241+ dead). No updated figures in Sunday sources. Uganda border closed. US Level 4 advisory active.
