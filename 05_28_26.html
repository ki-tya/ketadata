MAY 28 2026 · THU

Iran War · Day 89. CENTCOM carried out new strikes overnight against what it described as a site threatening US forces and commercial traffic. CENTCOM simultaneously posted to X accusing Iran of an "egregious ceasefire violation" — Iran used identical language against the US on Monday. Both sides have now filed mutual violation accusations. At Wednesday's Cabinet meeting Trump said: "We're not satisfied with it, but we will be. Either that, or we'll have to just finish the job." On sanctions: Iran will not receive sanctions relief in exchange for surrendering enriched uranium. On Hormuz: no state controls it — US will "watch over it." Iran maintains management would be coordinated with Oman. No deal signed.

Lebanon · separate track. Al Jazeera reports at least 16 killed and 58 wounded in Israeli strikes across southern Lebanon on Thursday. Six victims were members of the same family killed while fleeing on the Adloun Highway. Israel struck an apartment building in southern Beirut — first strike on the capital in three weeks. Lebanese Army soldier killed in Nabatieh. IDF has declared all areas south of the Zahrani River "combat zones." The US is reportedly pressuring Israel not to target Beirut over concerns it could derail peace talks between Israel and the Lebanese government.

Ukraine · Day 1554. Zelensky sent Trump an urgent letter on critical missile defense shortages. Overnight May 27 explosions reported near Voronezh bomber base — no confirmed attribution. Russia denied US diplomat visa to UN meeting; described it as "egregious disrespect."

Ebola. Figures as of May 26 (ECDC): DRC 105 confirmed (10 dead), 906 suspected (223 dead). Uganda 7 confirmed, 1 dead. Milan suspected cases ruled out May 25. No vaccine. No therapeutics. PHEIC active.

AI. KPMG deployed Claude to 276,000 employees across 138 countries. OpenAI launched DeployCo, a $4B consulting subsidiary. Cohere acquired Aleph Alpha — combined valuation $20B. Canada ruled OpenAI violated privacy law.
